About the Career Guidance and Counseling System

Welcome to our Career Guidance and Counseling System! This platform is designed to help students make informed career decisions by providing personalized advice, mentorship, and resources.

Our system connects students with experienced career counselors who guide them through various career paths, based on their individual skills and interests. With tools like the counseling diary and feedback system, students can track their progress and receive ongoing support as they navigate their career journey.

Our key features include:

  • Personalized career advice and mentorship
  • Counseling diary to track progress
  • Feedback system for continuous improvement
  • Resources and guides for different career paths
